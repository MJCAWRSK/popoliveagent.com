How to Register as a Poppo Live Agent

Follow these simple steps to become a registered Poppo Live Agent:

1

Download Poppo Live from the app store, playstore.

2

Create an account on the Poppo Live app.

3

Copy your Poppo ID from your profile.

4

Click the Agency Registration Link and enter your Poppo ID.

5

Click “Get” and check your Poppo messages for the verification code.

6

Enter the verification code and submit your registration.
